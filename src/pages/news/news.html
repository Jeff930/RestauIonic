Our Story

Our family of five immigrated to the United States in 1990. After struggling with various jobs, we managed to save enough to put up our own little restaurant in Los Angeles.

We wanted to offer the people of LA something unique so we decided to go with authentic Filipino cuisine. After all, the Philippines is a melting pot of various Asian cultures. Through the centuries, Filipino cuisine has evolved to incorporate the best of Oriental food.

We modeled our restaurant after a local Filipino eatery. And as a way of helping fellow Filipino immigrates in the City of Angels, we only employ Chefs who were originally from the Philippines.

With over 25 years of culinary experience, we are confident that there is no other place in LA that can offer the same authentic Filipino dining experience that we do.
