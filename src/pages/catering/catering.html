About Kusina Filipina

About Us

DISCOVER OUR STORY

Our family arrived at the City of Angels in 1990 in search of greener pasture. Shortly after, we’ve realized how much we missed the food back home in the Philippines. So we pooled all our resources to create our own restaurant.

We wanted our fellow Filipino immigrants in Los Angeles to experience authentic Filipino cuisine once more. We also wanted the rest of LA to get a taste of Filipino cooking.

The Philippines’ location makes it a melting pot of Oriental culture. Filipino cuisine has advanced by absorbing the best parts of other Asian cuisines. We were confident authentic Filipino food would not lose no matter the location.

We only employ Chefs who were originally from the Philippines. And our restaurant is designed to provide a traditional Filipino setting and ambiance.

Our 25 years of culinary experience guarantees that when it comes to providing an authentic Filipino dining experience, nobody does it better than us.

Arizenia
